Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers

About:

Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) is an international professional technical body of electrical engineers and people from associated disciplines. IEEE takes an active part in all the technical events held in the college. IEEE conducts various technical and non-technical fun events during the annual technical festival of KJSIEIT, Renaissance. It also conducts various seminars and workshops round the year, which help students gain an insight and equips them with the current needs and requirements of the industries.

Mission/Vision:

  • To foster technological innovation and excellence for the benefit of humanity.
  • To nurture and promote innovative technical ideas in the technical fields.
  • To solicit, refine and disseminate quality technical information.
  • To support personal development on all fronts in an individual.

ROLES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • To conduct technical education programmes for Human Research Development.
  • To develop a volunteered environment where technical thoughts leaders converge and create.
  • Communities for people working in various fields.
  • To organize technical gatherings to foster innovation and interchange.
  • To help students learn about new technologies in upcoming fields in an interesting and carefree manner, as well as help them groom themselves and their personality.
