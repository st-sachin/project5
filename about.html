About Committee Management System:

The topic of our project is the ‘College Committee Management System’, which will be helpful for managing all the committees in the college together and efficiently. This College Committee Management System will be managing all the data, the type of events, the upcoming events, and the past events of all the committees. This system will keep a record of the total strength of the committee, the total number of members who are currently present in the particular committee, and will also show the user if any slot/position is vacant in a particular committee and also the pre-requisites to join a particular committee. Thus this system will be very helpful for the user to get all the information of a particular committee and will also help the user in choosing the right committee according to his/her interests. Also, the committees will find the system useful as it will manage their data in a systematic and efficient format.

  • To inculcate ethical behaviour, responsibility and commitment among students.

Objectives of the Institute

  • To develop a ‘College Committee Management System’ for providing the user information about the various committees present in the college on a single platform. .
  • To enable the user to identify the committee he/she should join based on his/her interests.
  • To help the existing committees manage their data (i.e., number of members, vacant positions, upcoming events, and many more) in an easier and simplified way.
  • To enable the user to know about the upcoming and past events organized by the committees.
